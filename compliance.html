Audit & Compliance

Ensuring transparency and organizational excellence through rigorous compliance checks and structured audit frameworks.

The Audit Process

A transparent workflow from preparation to closure.

1

Preparation

Local committees prepare required documentation and evidence prior to the audit deadline.

2

Review

ECB conducts a thorough review of documents, policies, and exchange systems.

3

Findings

A detailed report is generated highlighting strengths, gaps, and areas of non-compliance.

4

Recommendations

ECB provides actionable steps to resolve gaps and improve delivery.

Closure

Final status granted upon successful implementation of all recommendations.

Compliance Framework

Exchange Standards

Monitoring the delivery of the 16 minimum criteria for every exchange.

Data Management

Ensuring GDPR compliance and secure handling of participant data.

Risk Management

Proactive identification and mitigation of legal and operational risks.
