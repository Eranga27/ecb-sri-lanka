Attraction Phase

Setting the right expectations is crucial. In this phase, marketing materials must be honest and aligned with actual national realities. No false promises.

Consideration Phase

Ensuring transparent communication during interviews, proper expectation setting regarding Job Descriptions (JD), and clear visa guidelines.

Preparation Phase

Delivering comprehensive Outgoing Preparation Seminars (OPS) and Incoming Preparation Seminars (IPS). Ensuring insurance and safety protocols are understood.

Realization Phase

The core experience. Ensuring 100% adherence to Exchange Standards: pickup, accommodation, first day of work, job description fulfillment, and continuous support.

Reintegration Phase

Closing the experience through Reintegration Seminars (RIS), feedback collection, and ensuring the participant has reflected on their leadership development journey.

Core Exchange Principles

The foundational pillars that protect our integrity.

Responsibility

Every member is responsible for the safety and quality of the experiences we provide.

Customer-Centricity

Putting the needs and well-being of our Exchange Participants and Partners first.

Accountability

Taking ownership of mistakes and actively working to resolve cases when standards are not met.
