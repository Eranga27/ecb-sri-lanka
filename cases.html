Case 1

Accommodation Mismatch

Situation

An Exchange Participant arrives at the host entity. Upon reaching the accommodation, they find that it differs significantly from what was communicated during expectations setting (e.g., sharing a room instead of a private room).

Question

What should the responsible member do in this scenario?

Answer: Verify the initial information provided on the portal, communicate transparently with the EP about the discrepancy, document all actions taken to rectify the situation, and escalate to the national ECB where required if an immediate alternative cannot be arranged.

Case 2

Unsafe Workplace

Situation

A participant reports to you that the working conditions at the Opportunity Provider's location are unsafe and violate standard labor regulations, making them feel threatened.

Question

What are the immediate steps that should be taken?

Answer: Assess the severity immediately. Prioritize participant safety by removing them from the environment if necessary. Escalate appropriately to the national ECB and MC, document all evidence and communications, and coordinate with the sending entity.
