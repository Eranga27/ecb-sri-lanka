About the Entity Control Board

Our mission is to build a culture of quality, accountability, participant safety, and exchange excellence across all entities of AIESEC in Sri Lanka.

Our Departments

The ECB operates through specialized departments to ensure comprehensive coverage of all quality and compliance aspects.

Education & Compliance

Developing learning paths, resources, and ensuring members understand AIESEC standards.

Audits & Compliance

Conducting regular audits to verify that LCs are adhering to standard operating procedures.

Customer Support & Case Management

Handling standard violations, participant issues, and providing swift case resolutions.

Digital Marketing & Comm.

Promoting the importance of quality and managing ECB's internal communication strategies.
