Understanding Exchange

How AIESEC Exchange develops leadership through international experiences.

Before Exchange

Preparation and planning phase where expectations are set, and participants are prepared for their cross-cultural experience.

During Exchange

Experience delivery and participant support. This is the realization phase where leadership development happens actively.

After Exchange

Reflection and leadership development. Participants debrief their experience and understand their personal growth.

Key Stakeholders

Exchange Participant (EP)

The young person taking part in the exchange to develop leadership qualities.

Sending Entity

The home AIESEC entity responsible for preparing and supporting the EP.

Host Entity

The destination AIESEC entity responsible for delivering the standards on ground.

Opportunity Provider

The organization or company providing the exchange opportunity.

Standards Center

The foundation of quality. Ensuring these standards are met guarantees a safe and impactful exchange experience.

Preparation Standards
  • Goal Setting: Establishing clear personal and professional goals before departure.
  • Expectations: Aligning expectations about the role, country, and culture.
  • Insurance: Ensuring the participant has valid health and travel insurance.
  • Visa Support: Providing necessary documents and guidance for visa application.
Experience Standards
  • Arrival Support: Being present at the airport or arranging safe pickup.
  • Accommodation: Providing safe, clean, and pre-communicated living conditions.
  • Safe Environment: Ensuring the workplace and living areas meet safety standards.
  • Job Description Alignment: Ensuring the actual work matches the opportunity portal description.
  • Learning Opportunities: Facilitating spaces for leadership development.
Completion Standards
  • Reflection: Conducting a debriefing session after the exchange.
  • Feedback: Collecting feedback to improve future delivery.
  • Leadership Development: Assessing the impact of the exchange on leadership qualities.
